# Backlog — the one canonical work queue

Sessions take items top-down within their surface. Claim before
working; one surface — one session at a time.

Format:
`- [ ] P1 | surface | task or defect, one line | ev:path (optional) | src:stage-NNN|audit-NNN|qa|owner | claim:tag (while being worked)`

- P0 broken for users / data loss · P1 broken flow or promise · P2 debt · P3 polish.
- `claim:` = dated session tag (`MMDD-topic`, e.g. `0713-hub`); remove on close or handoff.
- A claim dated older than a day with no progress trace is stale: take the item
  over and note the takeover — a crashed session must not block a surface.
- Done: check the box, leave the line; prune checked lines older than a month.
